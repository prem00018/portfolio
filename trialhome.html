About Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive. He is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC.

Bio:

Sundar Pichai was born on July 12, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. He earned his degree in metallurgical engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur and holds an MBA from Stanford University.

Pichai joined Google in 2004 and quickly rose through the ranks, becoming the product chief in 2014. In 2015, he became the CEO of Google, succeeding Larry Page. Under his leadership, Google has continued to innovate and expand its product offerings.

In 2018, Pichai became the CEO of Alphabet Inc., the parent company of Google. He has been instrumental in shaping the company's vision and strategy, and has been a key figure in the development of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
